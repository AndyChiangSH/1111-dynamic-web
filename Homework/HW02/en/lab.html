Signal and Image Processing Lab

Advisor Jiunn-Lin Wu
Location Room 347, Applied Science and Technology Building
Telephone 04-22840884#347
Website https://drphototw.wixsite.com/wujl/siplab
Introduction Referred to as "SIP Lab", the research focus of this lab is image processing, machine learning, machine vision, multimedia signal processing, image recognition, computational photography and other related fields. In addition to the research on related theories and algorithms, he also actively participates in related industry-university cooperation projects, hoping to contribute to the technological improvement of related industries through cross-disciplinary cooperation. At present, our main research directions are: 1. Image processing and machine vision, including image processing and deblurring technology, camera correction and optical defect detection applications, etc.; 2. Machine learning, including the theory and application of deep learning; 3. Multimedia signals Processing, including audio fingerprinting and video processing, etc.; Fourth, image recognition, including statistics and the design of neural network-like classifiers. 5. Computational photography; 6. High-dimensional image data analysis.
